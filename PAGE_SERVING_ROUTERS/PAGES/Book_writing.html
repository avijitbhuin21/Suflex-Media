Most-Asked Questions About Our Our Ghostwriting Services

Collected from 100+ Leaders During Our Discovery Calls

+ What If I Don't Like Your Content?

We offer unlimited revisions to ensure the content meets your expectations. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we'll work closely with you until the manuscript perfectly captures your vision and voice.

+ How long will your ghostwriters take to write a book?

Typically, a full-length book takes 4-6 months to complete, depending on the complexity and length. This includes research, writing, and multiple rounds of revisions to ensure quality and accuracy.

+ Do you help me with publishing after the book is written?

Yes! We can guide you through the publishing process, whether you choose traditional publishing or self-publishing. We have partnerships with publishers and can help with formatting, cover design, and distribution strategies.

+ How involved will I be with the book writing process?

You decide your level of involvement. Some clients prefer regular check-ins and chapter reviews, while others provide initial input and review the final manuscript. We adapt our process to match your availability and preferences.

+ Can I speak with the book writer directly?

Absolutely! We encourage direct communication between you and your assigned ghostwriter. This ensures your voice, ideas, and expertise are accurately captured throughout the writing process.

+ Will I own the copyrights to the book?

Yes, you will own 100% of the copyrights to your book. All intellectual property rights are transferred to you upon completion and final payment. You have complete ownership and control.

+ Do ghostwriters sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)?

Yes, confidentiality is paramount. All our ghostwriters sign comprehensive NDAs before starting any project. Your ideas, information, and the entire collaboration remain completely confidential.

+ Do I need to give credit to the book writers?

No, you don't need to credit the ghostwriter. The book is published under your name alone. Our writers work behind the scenes to help you achieve your publishing goals while you receive full authorship credit.
