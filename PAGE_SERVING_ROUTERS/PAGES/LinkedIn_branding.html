Most-Asked Questions About LinkedIn Branding Services

Collected from 150+ Professionals During Our Discovery Calls

+ How often should I post on LinkedIn?

Consistency matters more than frequency. We recommend posting 3-5 times per week to maintain visibility and engagement. Our team creates a content calendar tailored to your schedule and audience preferences.

+ Will the content sound like me?

Absolutely! We conduct in-depth interviews to understand your voice, style, and perspective. Our goal is to amplify your authentic voice, not replace it. You'll always have final approval on all content.

+ How long before I see results?

Most clients see increased engagement within 4-6 weeks. Building a strong personal brand is a marathon, not a sprint. We focus on sustainable growth and meaningful connections rather than vanity metrics.

+ Do you handle profile optimization too?

Yes! Profile optimization is included in our LinkedIn branding package. We optimize your headline, about section, experience, and featured content to maximize visibility and attract your ideal audience.

+ Can I review content before it's posted?

Yes! We provide content for your review and approval before posting. You have complete control over what gets published on your profile. We work collaboratively to ensure every post meets your standards.

+ Do you help with engagement and networking?

Yes! We provide engagement strategies and can help you identify and connect with key industry leaders. Building relationships is just as important as creating content.

+ What industries do you specialize in?

We work with professionals across various industries including technology, finance, healthcare, consulting, and entrepreneurship. Our team researches industry trends to create relevant, impactful content.

+ Is there a minimum contract period?

We recommend a minimum of 3 months to see meaningful results and build momentum. However, we offer flexible packages based on your goals and can discuss custom arrangements during our strategy call.
