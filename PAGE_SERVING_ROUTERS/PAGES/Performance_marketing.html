Most-Asked Questions About Performance Marketing

Collected from 100+ Businesses During Our Discovery Calls

+ What's the minimum budget for performance marketing?

While budgets vary by industry and goals, we recommend a minimum ad spend of $2,000-$3,000 per month to see meaningful results. This allows for proper testing, optimization, and scaling of successful campaigns.

+ How quickly will I see results?

Initial results can be seen within the first 2-4 weeks, but optimal performance typically requires 2-3 months of testing and optimization. Performance marketing is about continuous improvement and long-term growth.

+ What platforms do you advertise on?

We manage campaigns across all major platforms including Google Ads, Facebook/Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, and programmatic display networks. We choose platforms based on where your target audience is most active.

+ How do you measure campaign success?

We track comprehensive metrics including ROAS, CPA, conversion rate, click-through rate, quality score, and more. Most importantly, we align our KPIs with your business objectives—whether that's sales, leads, brand awareness, or customer lifetime value.

+ Do you provide regular reporting?

Yes! We provide detailed monthly reports with insights, recommendations, and performance analysis. You also get real-time access to dashboards showing campaign performance 24/7.

+ What if my campaigns aren't performing?

We continuously monitor and optimize campaigns. If performance isn't meeting targets, we quickly pivot strategies, test new approaches, and adjust budgets. Our data-driven approach ensures we identify and fix issues promptly.

+ Can you work with my existing marketing team?

Absolutely! We collaborate seamlessly with in-house teams, other agencies, and vendors. We can handle everything end-to-end or focus on specific areas where you need expertise and support.

+ What industries do you work with?

We've successfully managed campaigns across various industries including e-commerce, SaaS, B2B services, healthcare, education, finance, and real estate. Our strategies adapt to the unique needs and regulations of each industry.
