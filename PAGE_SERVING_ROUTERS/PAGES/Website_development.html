Most-Asked Questions About Website Development

Collected from 100+ Businesses During Our Discovery Calls

+ How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline varies based on complexity. A simple website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce sites or web applications may take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout development.

+ Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile, mobile-first design is a standard part of our development process.

+ Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) that allow you to easily update content, images, and more. We also provide comprehensive training and documentation to help you manage your site.

+ What technologies do you use?

We use modern, reliable technologies including React, Next.js, WordPress, Shopify, and custom solutions based on your needs. We choose technologies that ensure your website is fast, secure, scalable, and easy to maintain.

+ Do you provide hosting and maintenance?

Yes! We offer reliable hosting solutions and ongoing maintenance packages including security updates, backups, performance monitoring, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly.

+ Will my website be SEO-optimized?

Yes! We implement SEO best practices from the ground up, including optimized site structure, fast loading speeds, proper meta tags, schema markup, and mobile optimization to help your site rank well in search engines.

+ What if I need changes after the website is launched?

We offer post-launch support and maintenance packages. Whether you need minor updates or major features added, our team is always available to help. We also provide website evolution services as your business grows.

+ Can you redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns and modernization. We can refresh your design, improve functionality, enhance performance, and ensure your site meets current web standards while preserving your brand identity.
